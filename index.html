Problem Statement

Period poverty is problem that threatens the futures and opportunities of so many girls in Africa as over 12.5 million of the have to miss days of school because of natural bodily function. The problem has been often tackled by NGOs through the receiving of donations which is unsustainable as these donations are never enough to provide a constant supply of free sanitary to a large amount of girls. With no sustainable method of securing funding for these free sanitary pads many girls are left burdened by the impact school absenteeism and loss of opportunities especially as they are in the developing sections of their lives.


Eureka

OUR SOLUTION

We solve this problem of providing a sustainable and constant funding model for the free sanitary pads by funding these pads through Ad revenue. The pads will be delivered to the girls in schools, through the use of a vending machine and electric card. The Ad revenue will be generated from setting up digital ad screens in various partnered locations, and we form partnerships with these locations through the use of Corporate Social Responsibility tokens endorsed by Unicef and provided by the government to incentivise these business and establishments to allow our ad screens in their spaces. We would also be delivering these token to companies looking to advertise so they sign a contract to advertise through our screens for a period of time. We aim to provide constant funding for this sanitary pads all year long.
